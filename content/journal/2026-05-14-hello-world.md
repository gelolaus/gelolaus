## Why I finally built a blog

I've been putting this off for a while. Between community work, coursework, and side projects, "write more" always lived at the bottom of the to-do list. But here we are.

This space is for the things that don't fit anywhere else — longer thoughts on building technology, lessons from running communities, and the occasional rabbit hole I fell into at 2am.

## What to expect

Mostly tech. Sometimes community. Occasionally life.

I'll write about the things I'm actively working on — infrastructure, web dev, product thinking — and the lessons that come from shipping things and watching them break (or not break, which is somehow more suspicious).

If you're reading this, thanks for being here early.

— Gelo
